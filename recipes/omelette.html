Omelette

Description

The omelette is such a great dish beacuse it is healthy, delicious, and customizable. You can put pretty much anything you want in it, and by changing up your ingredients, you will never get bored.

Ingredients

  • 2 or 3 eggs
  • Cheese (I use American)
  • Vegetables (onion, zuchinni, tomato (yes, I will include tomato here), etc.)
  • Meats (bacon, turkey bacon, sausage, etc).
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. First, chop up and indredients you will use.
  2. Put some olive oil on a pan and put the heat to high.
  3. Put your vegetables or meats in, and cook them a decent amount.
  4. Then, crack and break your eggs into a bowl and beat them until they homogenous.
  5. Lower the heat of the pan to low or low-medium and pour the eggs in.
  6. With your spatula, push the egg and tilt the pan to get the raw eggs to cook. Keep doing this, and by doing this you will create layers.
  7. When there is a minimal amount of raw egg, raise the heat of the pan. Move the omelette around the pan to make sure it can slide and flip it.
  8. Lower the heat again and put cheese on the omelette.
  9. Once the bottom has cooked, fold the omelette in half and, voila, your omelette is ready to serve!
