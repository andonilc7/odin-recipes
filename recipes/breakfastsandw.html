Breakfast Sandwich

Description

This breakfast sandwich has the perfect combination of flavors, and it will be something that you will come back to time and time again.

Ingredients

  • Brioche Bun
  • Gouda Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Onions
  • Bacon
  • Sriracha
  • Mayonaisse
  • Butter
  • Olive Oil

Directions

  1. Slice your onion and caremelize it in butter on pan on the stove
  2. Preheat your oven to 375 or 400 degrees Farenheit and place the bacon on a pan with parchment paper and put it in the oven.
  3. Make your sriracha aioli by mixing 1 part sriracha to 2 or 3 parts mayonaisse.
  4. When the onions are done caremelizing, place them in a bowl and wipe the pan.
  5. Put some olive oil on the pan and put it on high heat.
  6. Crack one or two eggs onto the pan and lower it a tiny bit.
  7. When the eggs have cooked enough on the bottom, flip the eggs and turn off the heat shortly after.
  8. Put some gouda on top.
  9. Put the brioche in the toaster oven and put some gouda on it.
  10. Put some aioli on the bun, and assemble your sandwich in whatever order you like.
