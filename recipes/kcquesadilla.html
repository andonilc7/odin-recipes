KC Quesadilla

Description

This is the delicious KC Quesadilla, which it creamy, savory, and has the perfect touch of spice.

Ingredients

  • 2 Tortillas
  • Butter/Oil
  • Chicken
  • Andouille Sausage
  • Crab
  • Shallots
  • Pepper Jack Cheese
  • Spinach
  • Sour Cream
  • Pico de Gallo

Steps

  1. First, prepare the ingredients by cooking the crab, sausage, chicken, and shallots
  2. Prepare the pico de gallo
  3. Heat up a pan/griddle and put some oil and butter, and place the 2 tortillas down.
  4. Next to this, heat up the crab, sausage, chicken, and shallots with some oil/butter.
  5. Sprinkle enough cheese to cover each of the tortillas.
  6. Put some spinach and put the crab, sausage, chicken and shallots in.
  7. Close the quesadilla by placing the one tortilla on top, and press down a little to make sure it sticks.
  8. Cut the quesadilla into sixths, and present it nicely on a plate with some pico de gallo and sour cream on the side; at KC Prime, we put the pico de gallo on a leaf of radicchio.
